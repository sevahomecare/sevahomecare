About Us

Why Families Trust Seva Homecare

At Seva Homecare and Wellness Ltd, we believe that true care goes beyond basic assistance. It is about providing compassionate, personalized care that allows your loved ones to thrive where they feel most comfortable — at home.

Our team is composed of experienced, fully background-checked caregivers who are not only highly skilled but genuinely passionate about what they do.

We are proud to serve Calgary families with dignity, respect, and professionalism. When you choose Seva, you are choosing peace of mind.
